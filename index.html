ABOUT

In 2005, a dynamic group of engineers came together under the banner of the Local Government Engineering Development (LGED), marking the inception of a team dedicated to transformative development initiatives. Committed to enhancing local infrastructure and fostering sustainable growth, these LGED engineers brought a wealth of expertise to address the unique challenges faced by communities. Their collaborative efforts focused on implementing innovative solutions, such as upgrading roads, bridges, and other essential public amenities, thereby contributing significantly to the socio-economic development of the regions they served. Over the years, this dedicated team has become synonymous with progress, showcasing the impact that a group of committed engineers can have in shaping the landscape of local governance and community well-being. Thank you.
